Everything About Nightmare

Everything About Nightmare is a 2.5D horror game. It tells the story of a little girl named Alita who is trapped in a dark house, requiring Alita to get out of the house by passing several puzzles and hiding from monsters.

Role: Game Designer and Story Writer

You're The Prey

You're the Prey is a horror game for the PC platform. Players will shoot ducks while being watched by a mysterious entity called "the thing." The game is designed to create a disturbing experience with an eerie atmosphere.

Role: Story Writer

Ashes of Polyhymnia

Ashes of Polyhymnia is a game about how music affects mental health. It tells the story of a group of musicians trying to bring back the joy of life in a world overtaken by depression and emptiness.
